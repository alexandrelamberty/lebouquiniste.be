# Le Bouquiniste

A warm and welcoming website for **Le Bouquiniste**, an independent second-hand bookshop located in a charming old house filled with books, wooden shelves, and literary treasures.

The website reflects the atmosphere of the bookshop itself: authentic, timeless, and dedicated to the pleasure of discovering books.

## About

Le Bouquiniste is more than a bookshop.

It is a place where readers can wander among thousands of books, discover forgotten stories, browse rare editions, and enjoy the unique atmosphere of a traditional bookshop.

Every shelf has a story, every room invites exploration, and every visit offers the possibility of an unexpected discovery.

## Features

- Presentation of the bookshop
- Overview of book categories
- Photo gallery
- Visitor information
- Contact section
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices

## The Spirit of the Project

This website aims to capture the feeling of entering a traditional bookshop:

- Warm wooden interiors
- Shelves overflowing with books
- Quiet reading corners
- Vintage charm
- A passion for literature and heritage

## Visit

Whether you are searching for literature, history, philosophy, art, local heritage, children's books, or simply the pleasure of browsing, Le Bouquiniste welcomes curious readers and collectors alike.
